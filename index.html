Features

Gesture Recognition

Use hand gestures to mark flashcards as easy, hard, medium, mastered, and more - all without touching your keyboard.

Spaced Repetition

Optimize your learning with our intelligent SRS algorithm that schedules reviews based on your performance.

Chrome Extension

Seamlessly create flashcards from any text you encounter while browsing the web.

Easy Import/Export

Never lose your flashcards with built-in import/export functionality.
